Unit 3


In this unit, I chose to show the labs water and spiral. For water I made a main and jeroo method that together would cause the jeroo to plant flowers along the water on the first and last row of water. I made a squareside, side, and row method under jeroo methods to have the jeroo plant flowers along the water. When there was no water, I instructed the jeroo to continue hopping until it found water again. I needed two jeroos for the water lab. For the spiral lab, I made a main and jeroo code that would help with my repeating code. It told the jeroo to spiral around the red fences without running into them to get to the center of the spiral where the flower was.



Unit 4


In unit 4, I chose to show the labs alley run and recursive box. For alley run, I made a jeroo and main method that would detect when there was a flower in the island among the fences and water so the jeroo was able to pick it. The jeroo ended at the end of the island when all the flowers were picked. I used jeroo methods such as pickLeft and pickRight so the jeroo was able to detect the flowers around it and pick them. For the lab recursive box I used a simple main method that told the jeroo to begin the jeroo method that I made called method side. The jeroo was instructed to hop and plant flowers on the edge of the water all across the island.
